RAINBOW CAMPAIGN CORE VALUES

“Rainbow” is a natural source of color. Just like the abundance of the color spectrum in a rainbow,
it has a symbolic meaning of unity as a source of inspiration and hope to create the core values LEADS
for the youths of Barangay Baras

Leadership

Good governance in the Sangguniang Kabataan is essential for fostering a community in which young people may actively participate in local governance, voice their concerns, and contribute to the development of their communities. It guarantees that the SK acts as a positive change catalyst and a platform for youth empowerment and development.

Equity and Equality

Promoting social inclusion, equity, and gender development in the context of the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) is a critical mission that empowers young people to play an active role in building an inclusive, fair, and gender-sensitive community. This approach empowers youth to be actively engaged in local governance, championing a more inclusive and fair society where everyone's voice is heard and respected.

Active youth participation, employment, and livelihood development in the Sangguniang Kabataan are critical for building a future where young people are engaged, economically self-sufficient, and equipped with the skills and opportunities they need to succeed in their careers and contribute to the prosperity of their communities.

Accountability

Addressing environmental protection, climate change adaptation, and disaster risk reduction and resiliency in the Sangguniang Kabataan is vital for building a sustainable and secure community, where young people are actively engaged in safeguarding their environment and are prepared to face the challenges posed by climate change and natural disasters.

Focusing on peace and order, anti-illegal drug abuse, and public safety, the Sangguniang Kabataan plays a critical role in ensuring a safe and secure environment for youth to grow and thrive. By actively engaging in these areas, the SK contributes to a peaceful and drug-free community and equips young people with the skills and awareness needed to respond to various safety and security challenges.

Development

Education and academic development are cornerstones for the growth and development of young people. By actively focusing on these areas, the Sangguniang Kabataan contributes to the academic success and future prospects of the youth in the community, thereby fostering a more educated and skilled workforce and supporting the broader development of society.

Socio-cultural and sports development initiatives by the Sangguniang Kabataan enhance the quality of life for young people, contribute to cultural preservation, and promote physical well-being. These activities help create a vibrant, inclusive, and dynamic community where youth can celebrate their heritage, express themselves creatively, and maintain active and healthy lifestyles.

Service

Effective mass media, information, and communication in the SK not only keep young people informed about local developments but also empower them to voice their concerns, engage with the community, and participate actively in local governance. This helps create a more informed, connected, and engaged youth population that plays an integral role in community development.

Focusing on health and well-being, hygiene and sanitation, and nutrition, the Sangguniang Kabataan contributes to the physical and mental well-being of young people and the broader community. By promoting healthy habits, providing access to healthcare, and addressing nutrition needs, the SK helps create a healthier and more vibrant community where young people can thrive and reach their full potential.
